I remember going downstairs on Saturday mornings, clambering into bed with my parents. I'm told I would have a sippy cup full of V8 during these gatherings (I drank it so often the plastic interior of this cup was stained red). They'd put Transformers: Beast Wars on the TV. I guess my dad was checking things for work or something, because entwined with these memories is the unforgettable sound of his clunky laptop with a red ball for a mouse connecting to the internet via dial up.
